This site is a series of vignettes on Galton Watson (branching) processes, which are used to model population growth over time. The site contains three vignettes. The first presents an overview and the basic theory of single type Galton Watson processes. The second covers various methods of inference on a branching process's offspring distribution. The final vignette extends the basic theory to branching processes that contain individuals of multiple types.
